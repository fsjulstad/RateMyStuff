About



RateMyStuff is a website where you as a user can comment and rate diffrent products.

We are posting different products with professional reviews beside. The users can use a comment field under the product. In the comment field the users can say their opinion on the products, both positive and negative.

You will not be able to buy any of the products on RateMyStuff, it is only for commenting and rating. We in Team Rocket decided to make this site because we felt we wanted to help people that are unsure and wondering what kind of products they should buy. On our site they can see both professional reviews and what the other users opinions.
